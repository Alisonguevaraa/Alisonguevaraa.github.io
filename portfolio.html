Description of the Game

The story of the game is basically playing old pong but with different
sprites. This is a two player game where both sprites can move up, down, left,
and right to fight with the opposite player over the point. The type of
scoring that my partner and I implement was varable. The use of randomness
occurs when the ball sprite hits the sprites in order to continue the game.
The game gets more difficult once the two players start getting aggresive with
their sprite and try to get the point. The game ends once a player reaches
10 points and then it will restart.
